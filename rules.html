Disciplinary actions are final. However, individuals who are banned...

All information related to disciplinary actions is confidential...

Staff members are expected to uphold the highest level of professionalism both in and out of character...

Players who wish to dispute a staff action or decision may submit a support ticket...

Characters must be realistic and grounded within the world of Las Islas Filipinas...

Characters must reflect human personas. Non-human or supernatural characters...

Character names should be culturally appropriate, avoid offensive meanings, and fit within the setting...

Organization names must follow the same logic. Staff reserves the right to reject...

Players who are downed (unconscious) must not speak in VOIP...

Permadeath is only required when staff declares it after repeated violations...

All players must roleplay fear and value for their character's life...

Failure to roleplay appropriate fear in high-stakes or life-threatening scenarios...

RDM refers to initiating combat or killing another player without proper roleplay context...

Attacking or killing another player without prior verbal interaction...

Shootouts should be meaningful, story-driven, and not treated as casual or random events...

Public shootouts must consider civilian impact and server performance...

Metagaming is using information your character did not learn in character (IC)...

Sharing or acting on OOC knowledge IC is strictly prohibited...

Powergaming is forcing actions on others without giving them a chance to respond or roleplay...

Always allow room for other players to react and avoid actions that eliminate another player's agency...

Hogtie and lasso mechanics are powerful tools and must be used responsibly...

Captives must not break out unless there’s a reasonable chance...

Criminal groups are limited to 6 players in PvP situations...

If your gang is above the limit, communicate who’s involved before conflict begins...

Non-criminal and law enforcement groups are limited to 6 armed members...

Larger groups may gather for patrols or events, but only 6 may fight...

Bandit and criminal groups must have immersive storylines tied to the server's lore...

Group size is capped at 10, with mandatory use of in-character names...

Only one group may initiate conflict at a time for a specific reason...

Groups may defend themselves, but PvP participants are capped at 6 armed members...

Hostage situations must be organic and well roleplayed...

Hostages must be respected, not killed. Robbers and law enforcement must act within immersion...

Government workers (LEOs, staff, judges) must not engage in or assist criminal activities...

While characters can have attitude, they must uphold professionalism...

Robbery RP must involve strong verbal roleplay...

Victims must react with believable fear, but robbers cannot kill victims without deeper RP justification...

Only 4–6 robbers may participate. Robbers may only take items victims offer voluntarily...
